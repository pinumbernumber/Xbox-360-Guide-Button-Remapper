===============================================
To install Wireless Xbox 360 Remote Receiver
===============================================
1. Install the driver software here.
2. Plug the wireless receiver into one of your computer's USB ports.
3. If you receive an error such as "There was a problem installing your device", follow these steps:

	3.1 Go to Device Manager.
	3.2 You will see an "unrecognised hardware" section
	3.3 Right click on it and select "Update Driver Software".
	3.4 choose to manually select driver from the driver list.
	3.5 Search for Microsoft, then Xbox 360 controller.
	3.6 Sleect the Play and Charge Kit option.
	3.7 Click Install.
	3.8 You may need to reboot your computer for the changes to take affect.
